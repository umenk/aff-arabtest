Sidelights can compromise security if not properly secured. Opt for impact-resistant glass and a sturdy frame. Install a deadbolt lock, security bar, and peephole for added protection.

Sidelights allow ample natural light to flood your entryway. Choose the size and style of sidelights that optimize light without sacrificing privacy. Consider frosted or tinted glass for added privacy while still allowing light to enter.

Tip 5: Enhance Energy Efficiency

Look for doors with insulated cores and weatherstripping around the frame and sidelights to minimize heat loss and improve energy efficiency. This reduces energy costs and enhances the comfort of your home.

Regular maintenance ensures the longevity and optimal performance of your front door with sidelights. Clean the surfaces, lubricate moving parts, and inspect for any damage or wear. Prompt repairs will prevent minor issues from escalating into costly problems.
