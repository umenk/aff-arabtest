Front Door Makeover with Sidelights: Enhance Your Home's Curb Appeal

A front door with sidelights is a type of entryway that features a main door flanked by one or more narrow, fixed windows known as sidelights. This architectural element not only enhances the aesthetic appeal of a home but also provides additional natural light to the entryway and adjacent spaces.

Installing a front door with sidelights offers several benefits. First and foremost, it can significantly boost a home's curb appeal. A well-chosen and proportioned front door with sidelights can create a striking and inviting entryway, making a positive first impression on visitors and potential buyers. Furthermore, sidelights allow natural light to flood into the entryway, making the space feel more welcoming and spacious. This can also reduce the need for artificial lighting during the day, leading to energy savings.

Front doors with sidelights have been used for centuries in various architectural styles, from traditional to contemporary. In recent years, they have become increasingly popular due to their ability to enhance both the aesthetic and functional aspects of a home's entrance. When choosing a front door with sidelights, it is important to consider the overall style of the home, as well as the size and shape of the entryway. With careful planning and execution, a front door with sidelights can be a beautiful and practical addition to any home.
